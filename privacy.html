Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

1. Data Storage

Omega Vault stores collection data locally on the user’s device. Users maintain control over their data.

2. Account & Subscription Data

Account verification and subscription status are managed through Supabase and Stripe. Only minimal data necessary for authentication and subscription validation is stored.

3. Payments

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Omega Vault does not store full payment details.

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold. Data is used solely for account verification and subscription functionality.

5. Security

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect account-related information.
